This is a sample story that demonstrates the markdown format. Each story should have frontmatter (the content between the --- marks) that includes:

- title: The title of the story
- name: The name of the person
- coordinates: [longitude, latitude] for the map marker
- date: When the story was written
- location: The location name

The rest of the content can include regular markdown formatting:

## My Experience

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua. Ut enim ad minim veniam, quis nostrud exercitation ullamco laboris.
